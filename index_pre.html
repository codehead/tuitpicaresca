What is this?

Twitpanto is a panto on Twitter, taking place on the 20th of December 2010, at 2pm GMT.

How do I join in?

You don’t need to be a Twitterer to watch the fun, just come back on the 20th of December at 2pm GMT. But if you want to join in, then you’ll need a Twitter account – go and sign up, it’s free (there’s a guide to getting started). Don't forget to follow @twitpanto for updates.

On the day all tweets hashtagged #twitpanto will appear on the site (that means any messages containing the word #twitpanto with the # symbol). You can sign in to tweet directly from this site too using the button below.
